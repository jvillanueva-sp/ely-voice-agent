ELY Voice Agent

ELY Voice Agent is an internal business application for Eye Lash You, a beauty salon in Tampa, FL.

Purpose

This AI-powered phone system assists with appointment booking by checking staff calendar availability in real-time during customer calls.

Data Usage

The application accesses Google Calendar (read-only) to check appointment availability for 4 staff members. No calendar data is stored or retained.
