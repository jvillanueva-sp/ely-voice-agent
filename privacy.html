Privacy Policy - ELY Voice Agent

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

Overview

ELY Voice Agent is an internal business application used by Eye Lash You, a beauty salon in Tampa, FL. The application enables automated phone answering and appointment booking.

Data We Access

  • Google Calendar data (read-only): We access staff calendar availability to check open appointment slots during customer calls
  • Customer booking information: Names, phone numbers, and requested appointment times

How We Use Data

  • Calendar data is accessed only during active customer phone calls to determine availability
  • No calendar information is stored or retained after the call
  • Customer booking data is stored in our internal systems (Airtable) for appointment management purposes only
  • All data access is limited to authorized Eye Lash You staff members

Data Sharing

  • We do not share, sell, or distribute any calendar or customer data to third parties
  • Calendar access is restricted to 4 staff members' calendars only
  • Data is used exclusively for appointment booking operations

Security

  • All data transmission uses encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Calendar permissions are read-only
